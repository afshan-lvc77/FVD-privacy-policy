Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Welcome to Fast Video Downloader. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our latest version. Our primary goal is to provide a safe and simple tool for downloading media while respecting your privacy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. The app functions without requiring any user registration or account creation. All downloads are initiated manually by the user.

2. Core Features & External Links

Our app provides icons for easy access to Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, Moj, and X (formerly Twitter). Please be aware:

  • Clicking these icons may redirect you to external websites, official apps, or the Play Store.
  • We do not collect any data or login credentials from these third-party platforms.
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services.

3. Permissions We Request

To provide its core features, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage (Photos/Media/Files): To save downloaded videos directly into your phone's gallery.
  • Internet Access: To process links via our secure server and download video data.
  • Background Service: To ensure that your video download continues even if you switch to another app or minimize the downloader.
  • Notifications: To keep you updated on the progress and completion of your downloads.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses a secure GitHub-hosted server to process video links and retrieve the original file URL. No user data is logged or stored during this process. We currently do not use AdMob or any third-party tracking/analytics services.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, your identity remains 100% anonymous. We do not have any database to store your personal information.
