Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Welcome to Fast Video Downloader. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. Our primary goal is to provide a safe and simple tool for downloading videos while respecting your privacy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. The app is designed to function without requiring any user registration or account creation.

2. Permissions We Request

To provide its core features, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage (Photos/Media/Files): This is required to save the downloaded videos directly into your phone's gallery.
  • Internet Access: Required to access the links you provide and download the video data from the web.
  • Background Service: This allows the app to continue the download process even if you switch to another app or minimize the downloader.

3. Third-Party Services & Links

Our app uses a GitHub-hosted server to process video links and retrieve the original downloadable file URL. This process is automated and no user data is logged or stored on this server.

Please note that we currently do not use AdMob or any third-party advertisement services. If we include them in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being leaked from our side. However, we encourage users to be cautious about the links they paste into the app.

5. Notifications

The app may send local notifications to inform you about the status of your downloads (e.g., "Download Completed"). These are generated locally on your device and do not involve any external tracking services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
